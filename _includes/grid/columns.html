Column Wrapping

If the .-col* total exceeds 12 columns, the column groups which extend past 12 columns will wrap to a new line. If your goal is to always have a column wrap to a new line, it is suggested to create a row. In the example below, the last column (.-col4) wraps to a new line because column total is 13 (9+4). Since this exceeds 12 columns, the 4-column div wraps to a new line.
